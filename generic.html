Before you begin with accounting principles, it is important to understand what time of accounting you are dealing with.

There is managerial accounting and financial accounting

Managerial accounting (also called cost accounting) is about finding the costs of making products so that managers can decide how and what to produce with the smallest cost .

Financial accounting is about using financial statements to analyze a company. The people who use these financial statements are usually external auditors.
